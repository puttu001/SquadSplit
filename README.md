# SquadSplit

**Split expenses effortlessly. No subscriptions. No limits. Just fairness.**

SquadSplit is a free expense splitting app built for real people — friends on trips, flatmates sharing rent, colleagues splitting lunch. No premium tiers, no hidden charges, no account required to join a group.

---

## Who is it for?

- **Friend groups** planning trips, outings, or events
- **Flatmates** managing shared rent, groceries, and utilities
- **Colleagues** splitting team lunches or work trips
- **Anyone** who is tired of mental math and awkward money conversations

---

## What it does

- **Create groups** for any occasion — trips, households, events
- **Add expenses** with flexible splitting: equal, exact amount, percentage, or shares
- **Track balances** in real time — always know who owes what
- **Simplified settlements** — see the minimum number of transactions to settle all debts
- **Friend system** — connect with people you split with regularly
- **Activity feed** — full history of what was added and when
- **UPI integration** — generate payment links to settle directly
- **Email notifications** — OTP verification and expense alerts
- **Invite via link** — join a group without needing an account first
